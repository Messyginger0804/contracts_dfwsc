# Member Loan Agreement / Promissory Note

This Member Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **[Date]**, by and between:

**Lender:** Jeremy Ashley ("Lender")
**Borrower:** DFW Software Consulting, LLC, a Texas limited liability company ("Company")

---

## 1. Loan Amount

The Lender agrees to loan the Company the principal sum of **$1,300.00 USD** (the "Loan").

## 2. Purpose of Loan

The Loan is made for general business purposes of the Company, including but not limited to operating expenses.

## 3. Repayment Amount

The Company agrees to repay the Lender a total amount of **$1,500.00 USD**.

## 4. Repayment Terms

The Company shall repay the Loan in **one lump-sum payment** of $1,500.00 on or before **[Repayment Date]**.

(Alternatively, repayment may be made earlier without penalty.)

## 5. Interest

The parties agree that the difference between the Loan Amount ($1,300.00) and the Repayment Amount ($1,500.00) represents interest on the Loan.

## 6. Prepayment

The Company may prepay this Loan in whole or in part at any time without penalty.

## 7. Default

If the Company fails to make payment when due, the Loan shall be considered in default. Upon default, the Lender may demand immediate payment of the outstanding balance.

## 8. No Security

This Loan is unsecured. No collateral is pledged for repayment.

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of Texas**.

## 10. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding this Loan and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements.

## 11. Amendments

Any amendments to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.
